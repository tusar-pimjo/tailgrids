# Credits

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to everyone who has contributed to Tailgrids in any form. Your dedication and support have been invaluable.

Here are some of the key tools and resources we rely on include:

- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- [Inter font family](https://rsms.me/inter/)
- [Figma](https://figma.com/)
- [Lineicons](https://lineicons.com/)

All assets that are used to build Tailgrids are 100% free, MIT licensed, and Open-source our in-house tools to simplify licensing for ourselves and our users.

Finally, thank you to all Tailgrids users! Your feedback, suggestions, and continued usage drive us to improve.

Thank you for being a part of the Tailgrids journey!
